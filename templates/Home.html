CRISP-ML Lifecycle Framework

Automatic Classification of Municipalities According to Climate Vulnerability

An end-to-end Machine Learning ecosystem dedicated to analyzing, clustering, and ranking environmental emergency exposure across Colombian territories.

Core Mission

Climate Risk Detection: The primary objective of this system is to extract latent territorial vulnerability patterns. By processing thousands of historical emergency event logs with specialized Machine Learning architectures, the platform scales analytical capabilities from raw datasets into predictive territorial assets.

Project Context & Problem Domain

01 · Environmental Emergencies
Evaluates deep historical vectors for severe localized phenomena including regular flash floods, structural droughts, sudden storms, torrential flows, and intense vegetation fires over all municipal codes.
02 · Open Government Infrastructure
Utilizes open public datasets to retrieve standardized geographical attributes, socio-demographic baselines, and historical incident logging crucial to training robust classification models.

03 · Machine Learning Engines: Advanced multi-class classification and unsupervised spatial clustering models parse incoming records to identify parallel vulnerability profiles, setting up data-driven indicators for targeted resource optimization.

Implemented CRISP-ML Workflow

01
Business Understanding: Translating public environmental goals into explicit, quantifiable data operations and risk tiers.
02
Data Engineering: Constructing pipeline integrations, data parsing, geometric boundary resolution, and feature scaling.
03
Modeling: Iterating over classification ensembles and anomaly detection models to optimize territorial risk indexing.
04
Evaluation & Deployment: Cross-validating model accuracy against empirical damage indices and wrapping inferences into this dashboard system.

Active Core Dataset

UNGRD Source

Climate-Related Emergencies: Curated matrix holding comprehensive event tracking (floods, torrential surges, electrical damage, and fires).

Specifications

Format: High-throughput Tabular CSV ingested natively through secure programmatic Open API interfaces.
