Label Encoder Pipeline

Categorical strings submitted through this portal are converted into dense integers using the pre-fit rf_le_dept.pkl and rf_le_event.pkl dictionaries. This encoding ensures that textual definitions such as "Flash Flood" or "Landslide" are mapped exactly to the numeric tokens expected by the underlying decision trees.

Resampled Imbalance Compensation

The model utilizes a specialized operational state. To fix severe classification bottlenecks in data rows belonging to the Medium vulnerability class, the pipeline was exposed to a bootstrap up-sampling algorithm guaranteeing a robust decision boundary across all categories without compromising structural performance.
