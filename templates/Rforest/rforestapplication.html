Results Summary: The Random Forest model achieved 82.83% accuracy on the test set. The EVENT type was the strongest predictor (43%), followed by MONTH (14%) and MUNICIPALITY_EVENT_COUNT (13%), confirming that both the nature of the disaster and its temporal pattern are key drivers of climate vulnerability. Class imbalance in the Medium category was addressed with bootstrap oversampling, raising its precision from 15% to 49%.
