Phase Conclusion: The Logistic Regression model achieved 93.4% accuracy classifying 990 Colombian municipalities into Low, Medium, and High climate vulnerability levels. The top predictive features were PEOPLE affected, VULNERABILITY_INDEX, SEVERITY_INDEX, and AVG_SEVERITY_MUNICIPALITY. The High class showed lower recall (74%), suggesting future work with class-balanced methods or ensemble models.
